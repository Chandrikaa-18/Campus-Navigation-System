Campus Navigation

Campus navigation is all about making it easy for people to find their way around a college or university. This involves a mix of strategies, from detailed maps and well-placed signage to comprehensive directories and helpful wayfinding apps. Interactive information kiosks and guided tours, both in-person and virtual, also play a key role in familiarizing individuals with the campus layout and essential locations.
